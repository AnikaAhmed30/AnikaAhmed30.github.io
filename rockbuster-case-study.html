Rockbuster Stealth Data Analysis

Tools: Postgres SQL, Excel, Tableau and PowerPoint

Problem: Support Rockbuster Stealth LLC’s launch of an online video rental service to compete with streaming platforms like Netflix.

My Role: Conducted data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and visualization to deliver actionable insights for business decisions.

Process:

  • Queried and filtered multiple tables in Postgres SQL to extract relevant information.
  • Cleaned and summarized data in Excel to ensure accuracy and clarity.
  • Joined tables and used subqueries and common table expressions (CTEs) to analyze complex relationships.
  • Created visual dashboards and charts in Tableau to highlight trends and key performance indicators.
  • Compiled findings and recommendations into PowerPoint presentations for stakeholders.

Key Insights:

  • Identified underperforming regions that required operational adjustments.
  • Detected patterns in performance metrics that suggested optimization opportunities.
  • Highlighted high value customers and regions.

Impact: Delivered actionable insights and visual reports that enabled the Rockbuster Stealth team to make data-driven decisions and improve performance strategy.

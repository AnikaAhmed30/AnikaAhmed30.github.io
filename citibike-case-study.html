Citibike Data Analysis

Tools: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, kepler.gl, streamlit dashboard

Problem: Citibike aimed to understand usage patterns and optimize station locations to enhance operational efficiency and support urban planning in New York City.

My Role: Conducted data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and visualization to provide actionable insights for improving bike-sharing services.

Process:

  • Conducted data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and visualization to provide actionable insights for improving bike-sharing services.
  • Performed exploratory data analysis using Pandas to identify usage trends, peak hours, and geographic patterns.
  • Created visualizations with Matplotlib and Seaborn to highlight demand distribution and station performance.
  • Analyzed correlations between usage metrics and station locations to inform optimization strategies.

Key Insights:

  • Identified high-demand areas and peak usage times for better bike allocation.
  • Detected underutilized stations that could be relocated or optimized.
  • Highlighted factors most strongly correlated with successful station performance.

Impact: Provided actionable insights that enabled Citibike to enhance station placement, improve service availability, and support urban mobility planning.

Instacart Grocery Basket Analysis

Tools: Python, Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib

Problem: Instacart wanted to understand customer purchasing behavior to improve personalized marketing campaigns.

My Role: Performed data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and customer segmentation to uncover patterns in purchasing behavior.

Process:

  • Cleaned and preprocessed transaction data, handling missing values and duplicates.
  • Aggregated orders by customer and product categories using Pandas.
  • Created visualizations to identify trends in purchasing behavior with Seaborn and Matplotlib.
  • Segmented customers based on ordering frequency and product preferences.

Key Insights:

  • Frequent buyers preferred certain product categories.
  • Schedule campaigns on Tuesdays/Wednesdays.
  • Targeted campaigns could increase retention in high-value segments.

Impact: Provided actionable recommendations for personalized marketing strategies, helping Instacart improve customer engagement.

NYC Airbnb Data Analysis

Tools: Python, Pandas, Tableau, Matplotlib

Problem: NYC Airbnb hosts wanted to understand pricing trends and neighborhood patterns to optimize revenue.

My Role: Conducted data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and visualization to uncover actionable insights for pricing and occupancy strategies.

Process:

  • Cleaned and preprocessed Airbnb listing and transaction data, handling missing values and inconsistent entries.
  • Explored pricing trends by neighborhood, room type, and season using Pandas and Matplotlib.
  • Created interactive dashboards in Tableau to visualize key metrics such as average price, occupancy rate, and popular listing types.
  • Performed comparative analysis to identify underpriced or overpriced listings relative to market averages.

Key Insights:

  • Certain neighborhoods had consistently higher occupancy and could support premium pricing.
  • Private rooms were most popular in central Manhattan, while entire apartments were in demand in Brooklyn and Queens.
  • Seasonal trends indicated peak pricing opportunities during summer and holiday periods.

Impact: Provided hosts with recommendations to optimize pricing, increase occupancy, and improve revenue management strategies.
